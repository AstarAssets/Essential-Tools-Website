Adding a Smart Component (Aspect) to a Unity Object

  1. üö© Select Your Target Object:
    1. In the Unity Hierarchy window, click on the object you wish to add the Aspect Smart Component to.
  1. üö© Open the Inspector Window:
    1. Ensure the Inspector window is visible. If it's not, go to "Window" -> "General" -> "Inspector" in the top menu bar.
  1. üö© Click "Add Component":
    1. At the bottom of the Inspector window, you'll see a button labeled "Add Component." Click it.
  1. üö© Search for "Aspect":
    1. In the search bar that appears, type "Aspect."
  1. üèÅ Add the Aspect Component:
    1. The "Aspect" Smart Component should appear in the search results. Click on it to add it to your selected object.
    1. The Aspect component will now be attached to your object, and you can configure its settings in the Inspector window.
